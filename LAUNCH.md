# Kapture Forms · Launch Plan

Launch is staged. Soft launch to existing kooper customers first (warm channel, low risk). Public launch after Phase 2 ships with real Stripe.

---

## Soft launch (May–June 2026)

**Audience:** existing kooper · care customers and their HR contacts.

**Channels:**
1. Email to every kooper customer's HR admin: *"Your onboarding pack is now exportable to PDF / DOCX / CSV / Google Forms. Try the new flow."*
2. In-product banner on Rae's HR launcher and the kooper · hr deep-dive page.
3. Cross-link card on kooper-care.vercel.app/kooper-care-landing.html (Explore section).

**Offer:** £29 one-time download or £29/mo hosted, free for existing kooper customers for 90 days.

**Goal:** 20 active hosted licences from existing customers. Each one becomes a case study and a referral source.

**Tracking:** kooper:onboarding:submissions count, queue depth, export format usage breakdown.

---

## Public launch (Q3 2026, after Phase 2 ships)

**Audience:** UK care providers not currently on kooper.

**Position:** *"The staff onboarding pack the regulator wrote. £29 one-time, or run hosted with magic links and a queue."*

**Channels:**
1. **Direct outbound:** 50 letters to UK care providers in CQC's recently inspected list. Hand-typed, postal, with a printed sample PDF of the onboarding pack. (Ben Pearce-volunteer-style outbound — see strategy/02_PUBLISHER_PROGRAMME.md.)
2. **LinkedIn:** target HR managers and registered managers in care. Founder-led posts (Acie or named operator) showing the four pathways side-by-side.
3. **Care sector publications:** Care Talk, Caring Times, Home Care Insight — feature pitches positioning Kapture Forms as "the audit hash you've been missing".
4. **Referrals:** every existing kooper customer who refers a non-kooper provider gets a 50% discount for 12 months.
5. **CQC inspector outreach:** offer free inspector access to any inspector who signs in. They become case-study sources.

**Goal:** 100 paying licences across the three healthcare packs by end of Q3 2026.

---

## Marketplace launch (Q4 2026)

**Audience:** publishers (compliance officers, HR consultants, sector specialists, regulators-turned-consultants).

**Position:** *"You wrote the form once. Sell it forever. We host the platform. You take 70%."*

**Channels:**
1. **Personal outreach** to 50 named publishers identified in `strategy/04_FIRST_50_PUBLISHERS.md`. Phone call → DPA + sample pack → editorial review → live.
2. **CIPD, ACAS, CIM, Bar Council** — industry-body partnership conversations. Goal: a co-branded "compliance pack store" inside the parent body's site.
3. **Founder-led content:** a quarterly "Forms wrap" that profiles three publishers and their packs. Feeds LinkedIn + email.

**Goal:** 5 publisher packs live by end of Q4 2026, 1 publisher earning £1K+ MRR.

---

## Multi-industry launch (Q1 2027)

**Audience:** broader UK SaaS-adjacent buyers in HR, finance, legal.

**Position:** *"The forms layer your stack is missing. Pre-built. Audit-hashed. Exportable."*

**Channels:** as Q3 2026, plus:
1. **Industry-specific paid acquisition** — narrow Google Ads on regulator-named queries (e.g. *"AML KYC form template"*, *"safeguarding referral form"*).
2. **Partner integrations** — Bamboo, Breathe, Workday CSV import showcased on the marketplace landing as reference customer logos.
3. **Reviews + comparisons** — JotForm, Typeform, Google Forms feature comparison pages, SEO-led.

**Goal:** £25K MRR across packs, 30% non-healthcare by end of Q1 2027.

## Pricing rules during launch

| Window | Rule |
|---|---|
| Soft launch (kooper customers) | First 90 days free on hosted licence |
| Public launch (Q3 2026) | First 30 days free, no card up front |
| Marketplace launch (Q4 2026) | Publisher packs at 50% commission for first 3 months (instead of 30%) — bait the long-term 70/30 |
| Steady state (Q1 2027 onward) | Locked: £29 one-time, £29/mo hosted, 70/30 publisher share |

The £29 floor is non-negotiable. Discounts only via referral codes (50% off 12 months for kooper-referred customers).

## What kills the launch

- Slow PDF render. The PDF export is the primary deliverable; if it takes more than 3 seconds, buyers will think the rest is broken. Cache aggressively.
- A publisher pack that fails an editorial review post-launch. Right-of-removal clause is in the publisher contract for this reason.
- Stripe Connect payout delay. Publishers will leave loudly. Pay out monthly without exceptions; eat the float.
- Cross-contamination between Forms and kooper · care brands. The kooper customer who buys Forms must feel like they're buying a *different* product from a *single* studio. Forms must be defensible standalone for non-kooper buyers.

## Founder narrative (the line every channel echoes)

*"Compliance forms are the most underbuilt part of every UK SaaS stack. Operators hand-roll them in Word, lose the audit trail the moment the file leaves the browser, and rebuild from scratch when the regulator updates. We pre-engineered fifty of them. Pick yours, pick your format, ship today."*

That's the whole pitch. Write everything else in service of that.
