# Kapture Forms · Go-to-Market

The buyer-side playbook. Mirrors `LAUNCH.md` at a tactical level — channels, copy, weekly cadence, success metrics.

---

## The buyer

We are not selling to operators in general. We are selling to a **specific role**, in a specific moment, with a specific pain.

| Role | Moment | Pain |
|---|---|---|
| Registered manager (UK care) | Hiring 3+ staff this quarter | Inconsistent onboarding evidence; CQC finds a gap |
| HR admin (any sector) | Audit week | Has to recompile 18 onboarding files into a CQC pack in 3 days |
| Compliance officer | Regulator change | Scrambling to update 200 forms by the deadline |
| Owner-operator (multi-site) | New site opening | No process; needs templates yesterday |
| New hire | Day one | Has to sign 14 forms, doesn't know which are mandatory |

We sell the same product to all five. Different copy per channel.

## Channel one — direct outbound (postal, email, phone)

**Volume:** 50 letters / month, 200 emails / month, 30 calls / month.

**Cadence:**
- Week 1: postal letter (printed sample PDF, hand-typed letter, founder signature)
- Week 2: email follow-up
- Week 3: phone call
- Week 4: LinkedIn message if no reply

**List source:** CQC's most-recently-inspected providers (refreshed monthly), CIPD member directory, NHS Provider Trust HR contacts, Care England members.

**Conversion target:** 5% letter → email reply, 30% email reply → demo, 50% demo → trial, 30% trial → paid. Compound: 0.05 × 0.30 × 0.50 × 0.30 = **0.225% letter → paid**. So 50 letters → ~1 paying customer / month from this channel.

**Owner:** Acie + 1 named ops person. Not delegated to interns.

**SEO targets:** narrow regulator-named queries — *"AML KYC form template"*, *"safeguarding referral form"*, *"DoLS application form"*. Low volume, high intent.

**Owner:** Founder voice, ghost-edited if needed. Ben Heath / Mark Ritson / Hormozi cadence.

## Channel three — referrals (existing customers + sister products)

**Mechanic:** every kooper · care customer who refers a non-kooper provider gets 50% off for 12 months. Every Kapture Forms customer who refers a non-customer gets 1 month free.

**Why this works:** the buyer's network is dense. A registered manager in Birmingham knows three other registered managers. An HR consultant knows ten HR consultants. The referral tells the truth ("the audit hash is real") in the way no marketing copy can.

**Targets:** 25% of Q3 2026 customers are referred. 40% by Q1 2027.

**Owner:** referral codes auto-generated in the customer dashboard; ops team reaches out personally to high-engagement customers monthly.

## Weekly metrics (founder dashboard)

Every Monday morning Acie reviews:

1. **Sales** — last 7 days vs prior 7 days (count + £)
2. **Pipeline** — letters out, emails sent, demos booked, demos completed
3. **Conversion** — demo → trial, trial → paid, by source
4. **Active hosted licences** — net count vs prior week
5. **Publishers** — applications, in-review, live
6. **Content** — posts published, traffic, top 3 SEO queries
7. **Press** — placements live, in-pipeline
8. **Refunds** — £, count, reasons (every refund gets a debrief)

A weekly snapshot (4 lines max) goes into Notion with the date stamp.

## What we say no to

- **Free tier.** Every form pack has a £29 floor. The free tier attracts the wrong buyer and dilutes the publisher payout.
- **Custom development for one buyer.** We do not build bespoke forms. If a buyer needs custom, they hire a publisher directly off-platform.
- **White-label that hides Kapture entirely.** Co-branded yes (kooper · care, trade bodies). Fully white-label no — the audit hash trail must remain attributable.
- **Long-tail SEO content farm.** No 500 thin SEO pages. We publish 1 deep piece a week, not 5 shallow ones.
- **Conference sponsorships before Q3 2027.** ROI is unproven for our buyer profile. Direct outbound + content + referral first.
