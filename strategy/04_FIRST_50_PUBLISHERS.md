# Kapture Forms · First 50 Publishers

The named list. These are the first 50 we personally invite to publish on Kapture Forms. Outreach starts Q4 2026, all signed by Q1 2027.

---

## How to read this list

Every entry is a **role + sector + signal**, not a fixed name. Names get filled in as outreach progresses. The signal column says *why this person*. The status column tracks where they are in the pipeline.

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `target` | identified, not yet contacted |
| `outreach` | first contact made, no reply yet |
| `engaged` | reply received, in conversation |
| `applied` | formal application submitted |
| `review` | editorial review of their pack in progress |
| `live` | pack is live on the marketplace |
| `pass` | declined or unfit, do not re-engage |

## Outreach script (template)

```
Subject: We're building a compliance-form marketplace and we want you on it

Hi [name],

I'm Acie at Kapture. We've built a curated marketplace of pre-built
compliance forms — the kind you and I have written 200 times for
different clients. The first product is live for UK care providers.

I want you to be one of the first 50 publishers. The deal:

· You list your form pack, we host the platform.
· 70% of every sale, paid monthly on the 1st, no minimum balance.
· We supply free DPA + indemnity templates, free editorial mark-up.
· Your bio + credentials get a profile page. We feature 3 publishers
  per quarter in our outbound.

Most relevant for you: [the specific pack you've watched them write]

Would you take a 20-minute call this week to walk through it?

— Acie
```

Postal version: hand-typed, printed sample of the live onboarding pack PDF, signed by Acie. Sent in a black envelope with the Kapture sun on the back.
